The survival status of a Witness.

Entities relying on this vocabulary:

  • Witness
Term Description Reference
complete All of the literary content (not including dedication, etc.) is extant and legible. NA
defective Some pages containing literary content are missing from the extant witness, but the majority or a significant portion survives. NA
fragment Only 1 or 2 pages are all that remain of the witness, which is expected to be much longer. NA
lost In the case of a hypothetical witness, whose existence is posited, no part of the witness survives. NA
