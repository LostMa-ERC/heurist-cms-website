Definition

A claim that attests to the existence of an entity, which is lost or suffers from a dearth of archival evidence.

Relationships

Relationships that are registered on the Hypothesis entity itself / originating from it.

  • Required
    • Hypothesis must relate to 1 Text, Witness, or Document
  • Optional
    • Hypothesis can relate to 0, 1, or many Citation
