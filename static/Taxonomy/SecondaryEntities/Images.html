Definition

A range of image files from a Digitization, which correspond to a discrete, uninterrupted series of pages or leafs of a Part of a Document.

Relationships

Relationships that are registered on the Images entity itself / originating from it.

  • Required
    • Images must relate to 1 Part
    • Images must relate to 1 Digitization
  • Optional

Attributes

Name Definition Data type Repeatable Necessity Refers to entity Defined by vocabulary
represents_pages The portion (pages) of the document represented in the digitized images. Foreign key No Required Part NA
contained_in The digital object, which has a URL, from which this discrete range of images comes. Foreign key No Required Digitization NA
file_range A formatted string that represents the index of the first and last image file in the series of images.
When multiple files, separate the first and last with two colons, i.e. ""4::725"".
When a single file, do not include the separator, i.e. ""725"". 		Text (single line) No Required NA NA
corresponding_page_numbers A formatted string that represents the numbering at the start and end of the corresponding series of pages.
When a range of pages, separate the two numbers with two colons, i.e. ""1r::4v"".
When a single page, do not include separator, i.e. ""1r"". 		Text (single line) No Required NA NA
