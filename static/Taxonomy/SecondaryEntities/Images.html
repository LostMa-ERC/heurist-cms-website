Definition

A range of image files from a Digitization, which correspond to a discrete, uninterrupted series of pages or leafs of a Part of a Document.

Relationships

Relationships that are registered on the Images entity itself / originating from it.

  • Required
    • Images must relate to 1 Part
    • Images must relate to 1 Digitization
  • Optional
