Definition

A manifestation of a Text in a determined and legible sequence of characters, which have been inscribed on a physical document.

Relationships

Relationships that are registered on the Witness entity itself / originating from it.

  • Required
    • Witness must relate to 1 Text.
    • Witness must be related to 1 term from Status.
  • Optional
    • Witness can relate to 0, 1, or many Part.
    • Witness can relate to 0 or 1 Witness.
    • Witness can relate to 0, 1, or many Document.
    • Witness can relate to 0, 1, or many Person.
    • Witness can relate to 0, 1, or many Place.
    • Witness can relate to 0, 1, or many Citation .
Example of Hypothetical Version

According to a fourteenth-century inventory of a library at the Abbey of Peterborough, there was a Witness to the French Text chanson d'Otinel in the monks' collection; however, the inventoried manuscript no longer exists.

When creating the Witness entity, set "is hypothetical" to "True" and create a Hypothesis entity, shown below, that links to the lost chanson d'Otinel and details the claim attesting to its existence. The hypothetical Witness can be related to more than one Hypothesis, which has its own degree of certainty and draws on its own sources of information to make the argument.

Hypothesis entity
Entity attribute Example description
attested object This example; the lost, hypothetical Witness. (Witness foreign key)

certainty 3
