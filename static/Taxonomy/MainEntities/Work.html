Definition

An abstract 'work', as an entity of superior level to a given text. Typically, it can be a 'geste' known by several major versions or independent texts, like the Story of Guillaume or Vivien, or Perceval for that matter.

Relationships

Relationships that are registered on the Work entity itself / originating from it.

  • Required
    • Work must be related to 1 term from Matter.
  • Optional
    • Work can relate to 0 or 1 Cycle .
    • Work can relate to 0 or 1 Work
    • Work can relate to 0, 1, or more Citation .
