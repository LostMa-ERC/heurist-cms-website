Recommended Workflow

A literary work's tradition can be recorded through 6 entities. There are more entities available in the data model, which can (and sometimes should) be used to enrich the tradition's components.

When entering the tradition into the database, we recommend you start from the abstract literary content, the cycle or work, and proceed toward the concrete, the document and its parts.

  1. Cycle
  2. Work
  3. Text
  4. Witness
  5. Document
  6. Part
